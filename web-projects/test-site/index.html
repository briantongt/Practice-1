The bunnies

Rabbits are small, furry mammals with long ears, short fluffy tails, and strong, large hind legs. They have 2 pairs of sharp incisors (front teeth), one pair on top and one pair on the bottom. They also have 2 peg teeth behind the top incisors. Their teeth are specifically adapted for gnawing and grow continuously throughout their lives. There are some kind of bunnies>

  • American Bunny
  • Belgian Hare Bunny
  • Blanc de Hotot

Rabbit breeds come in all shapes, sizes, colors, and personalities, which can make picking the right one overwhelming.
